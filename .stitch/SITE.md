# Site Vision: Mechanistic Interpretability Research & Portfolio

## 1. Vision
The Digital Forensic Study. A high-end, digital laboratory hidden within a Victorian library. It serves as a personal portfolio, a repository for mechanistic interpretability research, and a showcase for fun side projects. The aesthetic is "Sherlock Holmes / Dr. House diagnostician" applied to neural network internals.

## 2. Target Audience
ML researchers, collaborators, tech enthusiasts, and anyone interested in the inner workings of AI models or creative coding projects.

## 3. Core Capabilities
- Presenting a professional "Curriculum Vitae" / About Me
- Publishing mechanistic interpretability case studies
- Showcasing fun personal coding projects and experiments
- Providing a digital "lab notes" space for ongoing thoughts
